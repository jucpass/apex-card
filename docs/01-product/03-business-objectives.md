# Business Objectives

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Approved

## Primary Objective
Develop a secure, scalable platform enabling Diaspora Invest to manage members, partners and subscriptions.

## Operational Objectives
- Register members and partners.
- Manage subscriptions.
- Validate memberships with QR codes.
- Centralise administration.
- Moderate reviews.
- Support multilingual operation.

## Technical Objectives
- API-first architecture.
- Modular backend.
- PostgreSQL + Prisma.
- TypeScript.
- Docker-ready deployment.
- Multi-country support.
- Future extensibility with minimal refactoring.

## Out of Scope
Marketing, partner acquisition, member acquisition and commercial KPIs are managed by Diaspora Invest.
